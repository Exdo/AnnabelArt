Activities

I have always worked in various schools and art groups teaching people of all ages. When my own children were young I was fascinated by the natural creativity and enthusiasm of children which was then followed by the awkward stumbling blocks of the teenage years.

This later developed into involvement with adults helping them overcome their fears and encouraging them to find their own artistic path. For the last six years I have been participating with adults in painting sessions.
